Our Services

Real-Time Road Mapping

Colour-coded live maps showing pothole locations, rough road sections, speed bumps, and impassable areas across Kenya.

Driver Behaviour Scoring

Automatic 0–100 scoring based on harsh braking, sharp cornering, speed violations, and road impact events.

Fleet Management

Fleet owners can register multiple drivers, track their vehicles in real time, and monitor safety scores from one dashboard.

Pothole Detection

MPU6050 accelerometer detects vertical g-force spikes above configurable thresholds, classifying events into 4 severity levels.

Obstacle Detection

Ultrasonic sensor detects flooded roads, impassable barriers, and obstacles within configurable range thresholds.

Historical Analytics

Full event logging with timestamped GPS coordinates for road authority reporting and infrastructure planning.
