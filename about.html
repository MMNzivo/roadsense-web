Our Mission

Transforming how Kenya understands its roads

Every day, millions of Kenyans drive on roads that are riddled with potholes, speed bumps, and hazardous sections — without any real-time information about what lies ahead.

RoadSense changes that. By placing smart sensors in vehicles, we crowdsource a continuously updated map of road conditions across the country — benefiting every driver, every fleet operator, and every road authority in Kenya.

What We Offer

Live Road Condition Mapping

A continuously updated, colour-coded map showing the severity of road conditions across Kenya — from smooth tarmac to critical potholes — updated in real time as vehicles travel.

Driver Behaviour Scoring

Every registered driver receives a personalised 0–100 safety score that reflects their real driving behaviour — harsh braking, sharp cornering, excessive speed, and more.

Fleet Management

Fleet and taxi operators manage all their vehicles and drivers from a single dashboard — tracking locations, scores, and road events across their entire fleet simultaneously.

Adaptive Road Intelligence

Our detection system learns from road conditions in real time — automatically adjusting sensitivity to distinguish genuine potholes from normal road texture and engine vibration.

Hazard Alerts

When a vehicle approaches a known hazard — flooded road, deep pothole, or impassable section — the system raises an immediate alert so the driver can respond safely.

Road Authority Reporting

Aggregated road condition data — with GPS coordinates and severity classifications — is made available to road authorities and county governments to guide infrastructure repairs.

How RoadSense Works
1

Install Device

A compact sensor unit is installed in your vehicle. It powers on automatically and connects to the internet.

2

Drive Normally

The device monitors motion silently in the background. No action needed from the driver — it only activates when the vehicle is moving.

3

Events Detected

Road impacts, driver behaviour events, and GPS position are detected and classified automatically every few seconds.

4

View Your Data

Log in to the RoadSense dashboard to see your driving score, road condition map, and full event history updated in real time.
